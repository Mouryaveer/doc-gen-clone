\section{ASSIGNMENT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY}

\textbf{2.1 Assignment.} The Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to Turn2Law, with full title guarantee, all right, title, and interest in and to the Assigned IP worldwide, including all present and future rights to exploit, use, reproduce, modify, distribute, sublicense, and create derivative works from the Assigned IP.

\textbf{2.2 Work Product.} All Work Product shall be and remain the sole and exclusive property of Turn2Law from the moment of creation. To the extent any Work Product constitutes a ``work made for hire'' under applicable law, it shall be owned by Turn2Law. To the extent it does not automatically vest in Turn2Law, the Assignor hereby assigns all rights therein to Turn2Law pursuant to Clause~2.1.

\textbf{2.3 Future IP.} The assignment in Clause~2.1 shall apply to all Assigned IP created during the Term of this Agreement, including IP that is conceived or reduced to practice after the termination of this Agreement but which arose from work performed during the Term.

\section{MORAL RIGHTS}

To the extent permitted by applicable law, the Assignor hereby waives all moral rights (including rights of attribution and integrity) in the Assigned IP and the Work Product in favour of Turn2Law and its successors and assigns.

\section{PATENT RIGHTS}

\textbf{4.1} The Assignor agrees to promptly disclose to Turn2Law all inventions, discoveries, and innovations made during the Term, whether or not they are believed to be patentable.

\textbf{4.2} The Assignor agrees to execute, at Turn2Law's request and expense, all documents necessary to file, prosecute, obtain, maintain, and enforce patent rights in the Assigned IP in any jurisdiction.

\section{TRADEMARK RIGHTS}

All trade names, trademarks, service marks, and brand assets developed by the Assignor in connection with Turn2Law's business shall vest exclusively in Turn2Law. The Assignor shall not register or attempt to register any such marks in its own name.

\section{COPYRIGHT}

All original works of authorship, including software, documentation, creative works, and marketing materials, created by the Assignor in connection with Turn2Law's business shall be owned exclusively by Turn2Law. The Assignor hereby assigns all copyright in such works to Turn2Law pursuant to the Copyright Act, 1957.

\section{CONFIDENTIALITY}

\textbf{7.1} The Assignor acknowledges that in the course of the engagement, the Assignor will have access to Confidential Information of Turn2Law. The Assignor agrees to: (a) maintain such information in strict confidence; (b) not disclose it to any third party without Turn2Law's prior written consent; and (c) use it solely in connection with the performance of the Assignor's obligations.

\textbf{7.2} This obligation shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of five (5) years.

\section{EXCEPTIONS}

The assignment under Clause~2 shall not apply to IP that: (a) was developed entirely on the Assignor's own time without using Turn2Law's resources, facilities, or Confidential Information; and (b) does not relate to Turn2Law's current or reasonably anticipated business, research, or development activities. The Assignor shall bear the burden of establishing these exceptions.

\section{TERM}

\textbf{9.1} This Agreement shall be effective from the Effective Date and shall continue for \textbf{{{Term}}} or for the duration of the Assignor's engagement with Turn2Law, whichever is longer.

\textbf{9.2 Survival.} Clauses~2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, and 10 shall survive the termination or expiry of this Agreement.

\section{REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES}

The Assignor represents and warrants that: (a) the Assignor has the full right and authority to enter into this Agreement and to make the assignments herein; (b) the Assigned IP does not infringe the IP rights of any third party; (c) there are no liens, encumbrances, or claims on the Assigned IP; and (d) the Assignor has not previously assigned, transferred, or licensed the Assigned IP to any third party.
