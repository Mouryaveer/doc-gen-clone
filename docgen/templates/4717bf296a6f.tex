% ── Recitals ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section*{RECITALS}

WHEREAS the Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship (\textbf{"Purpose"}), in the course of which Turn2Law may disclose certain confidential and proprietary information to the Recipient; and WHEREAS the Parties desire to set out the terms and conditions governing such disclosure; NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein and for other good and valuable consideration, the Parties agree as follows:

% ── Clause 1 ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{DEFINITIONS}

\textbf{1.1 ``Confidential Information''} means any and all information or data, in any form or medium, disclosed by Turn2Law to the Recipient -- whether orally, in writing, electronically, or otherwise -- that (a) is designated confidential at the time of disclosure; or (b) a reasonable person would consider confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure. Confidential Information includes without limitation: business plans, financial data, customer lists, marketing strategies, technical data, software, source code, trade secrets, research, and all information relating to the Platform.

\textbf{1.2 ``Platform''} means Turn2Law's integrated legal-tech solution offering AI-powered document drafting, online lawyer consultancy, intelligent legal chatbots, and specialised startup legal services.

\ifthenelse{\equal{nothing is confidential}{}}{}{%
\textbf{1.3 Specific Confidential Information:} nothing is confidential
}

% ── Clause 2 ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{OBLIGATIONS OF THE RECIPIENT}

\textbf{2.1} The Recipient agrees to: (a) hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence; (b) not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent of Turn2Law; (c) use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose; (d) protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care applied to its own confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care; and (e) restrict access to those employees, agents, or advisors who have a legitimate need to know and are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations.

\textbf{2.2 Non-Use.} The Recipient shall not use, exploit, or commercially benefit from the Confidential Information for any purpose other than the Purpose.

% ── Clause 3 ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{EXCLUSIONS}

The obligations under Clause 2 shall not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement; (b) was rightfully known to the Recipient prior to disclosure as evidenced by written records; (c) is independently developed by the Recipient without reference to the Confidential Information; (d) is received from a third party free from any obligation of confidentiality; or (e) is required to be disclosed by applicable law or governmental authority, provided the Recipient gives Turn2Law prompt prior written notice and cooperates with Turn2Law in seeking a protective order.

% ── Clause 4 ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{TERM AND SURVIVAL}

\textbf{4.1} This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for \textbf{3 (three) years}, unless earlier terminated by either Party on thirty (30) days' written notice.

\textbf{4.2 Survival.} Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination for three (3) years or for as long as the Confidential Information retains its confidential character, whichever is longer.

% ── Clause 5 ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{RETURN OR DESTRUCTION OF INFORMATION}

Upon termination or upon written request by Turn2Law, the Recipient shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information and any copies, extracts, or derivatives thereof, and shall certify such return or destruction in writing.

% ── Clause 6 ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY}

Nothing in this Agreement grants the Recipient any right, title, or interest in or to the Confidential Information or any intellectual property therein. All Confidential Information remains the exclusive property of Turn2Law.

% ── Clause 7 ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{REMEDIES}

The Recipient acknowledges that any breach may cause irreparable harm to Turn2Law for which monetary damages would be inadequate. Accordingly, Turn2Law shall be entitled to seek equitable relief, including injunction and specific performance, in addition to all remedies available at law or in equity, without requirement to post any bond or security.

% ── Clause 8 ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{DISCLAIMER}

All Confidential Information is provided ``as is.'' Turn2Law makes no warranties, express or implied, as to the accuracy, completeness, or fitness for any particular purpose of the Confidential Information.
